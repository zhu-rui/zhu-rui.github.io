Bio

ABOUT ME

I have been with the National Pilot School of Software, Yunnan University, since 2016/8. I am a visiting researcher with School of Information Science and Technology, Peking University from 2019/9-2020/6.

I now work as the secretary of the Software Engineering Teaching Committee of Yunnan Province, a core member of the Key Laboratory of Software Engineering in Yunnan Province, an important member of the data-driven software engineering provincial science and technology innovation team of Yunnan University, an executive director of the Journal of Computer Integrated Manufacturing, and a reviewer of several international SCI journals.

My research interests are concentrated on Data-driven Intelligent Software Engineering, Mining Software Repository, Process Mining, Software Development Micro-Process (SDMiP), Big-Data on Traffic et al. I work with both graduate and undergraduate students. If you find our recent work interesting and enjoyable mining data or making real-world data analytics (in advanced process mining, data mining, and machine learning techniques), email me with your CV for opportunities.
